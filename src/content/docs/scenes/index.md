## Step 8 — The Scene List

*Go through your four-page synopsis and list every scene required to tell the story. For each scene, note whose POV it's in, where it takes place, what the conflict is, and what changes by the end. A scene where nothing changes is not a scene — it is summary.*

:::note[The scene test]
Every scene must move at least one of these: the external plot, a character's internal arc, or the reader's understanding of the stakes. If a scene moves none of these, cut it.
:::
