## Step 5 — Character Synopses

*Write a one-page synopsis of the story from the point of view of each major character. This is not a character biography — it is the plot of your novel as that character experiences and drives it. Write in third person, past tense.*

:::note[Why this matters]
This step reveals whether your secondary characters have real agency. If a character's synopsis reads as "they watched the protagonist do things," that character is a prop, not a person. Give them desires that conflict with the protagonist's — that's where dramatic tension lives.
:::
