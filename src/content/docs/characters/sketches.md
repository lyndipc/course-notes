## Step 3 — Character Sketches

*For each major character, fill out the template below. Focus on the character's inner life — their motivation, their flaw, and the moment of transformation. Minor characters only need a name and role for now.*

:::tip[Snowflake guidance]
Do this for your protagonist and every major character before expanding the synopsis. Characters drive plot. If your plot has a structural problem, it usually means a character motivation isn't clear.
:::
