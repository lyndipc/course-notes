## Step 7 — Character Detail Charts

*Expand your character sketches into full character descriptions. You should now know the story well enough to understand exactly who these people are — their history, their voice, their contradictions.*

:::tip[Scope]
Don't turn this into an endless list of hair color and zodiac signs. Focus on the details that affect behavior: formative experiences, beliefs, relationships, and secrets. Include a physical description only if appearance is narratively significant.
:::
